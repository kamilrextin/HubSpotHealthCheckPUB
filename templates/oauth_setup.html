Setting Up Your HubSpot App

Follow these steps to enable "Login with HubSpot" authentication for secure, one-click access.

1

Create a HubSpot App

  1. Go to developer.hubspot.com
  2. Sign in with your HubSpot account
  3. Click "Create app" or navigate to "Manage apps"
  4. Create a new app (name it something like "Marketing Ops Audit Tool")
2

Configure OAuth Settings

Set Redirect URL:

http://workspace.kamilrextin.replit.app/oauth/callback

Copy this exact URL into your app's redirect URL field (using HTTP for development)

Required Scopes:

oauth (Always required)
crm.objects.contacts.read
crm.objects.companies.read
crm.objects.deals.read
crm.objects.listings.read
crm.schemas.listings.read
forms
automation
automation.sequences.read

Perfect! These scopes match what you have configured in your HubSpot app. The OAuth flow will now work correctly.

3

Get Your App Credentials

Client ID & Client Secret

Once your app is created, you'll find these credentials in the app's "Auth" tab. The Client ID is public, but keep the Client Secret secure.

4

Ready to Connect!

Once you've set up your HubSpot app with the correct settings above, you'll be able to use the "Login with HubSpot" button for seamless authentication.
